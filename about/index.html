Who We Are

Ahmad Hasan

Founder · Robotics Lead

Ahmad is a self-taught maker who blends Python, ROS 2, metal-work and astrophysics to turn raw ideas into autonomous machines. He spent six years at Dubai Astronomy Group fabricating full-automatic observatory domes and writing the UAE’s first meteor-detection ML pipeline — then built Merhaab, a Jetson-powered receptionist robot that greeted 20 000 visitors at GITEX.

  • Designer / Astronomer since 2013 (Dubai Astronomy Group)
  • Ex-Software Engineer, Sharjah Academy for Astronomy & Space Sciences (2017-19)
  • Projects: UAEMeteor Network, decimetric radio telescope recorder, two telescope pier builds
  • Skills: Python · C++ · PCB design · TIG welding · 3-D printing · ML model training
  • Published at COSPAR 2021 and eMeteorNews 2019

Faisal Al-Saleh

Sales Manager

Faisal turns bleeding-edge demos into signed POs. With a background in GCC retail roll-outs he now matches NGOs, malls and event organisers with robots that deliver measurable ROI in foot-traffic, dwell-time and conversions.
