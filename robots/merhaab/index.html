Merhaab Prototype

Al Maarifa-funded receptionist that started it all

Status: Decommissioned – archival unit

Merhaab was our very first build — a privately funded proof-of-concept sponsored by Al Maarifa Electronics. Entirely 3-D-printed around an off-the-shelf mannequin frame, it proved that expressive, mobile reception robots could be fabricated on a shoestring.

Build snapshot

  • ROS Noetic running on an Nvidia Jetson Nano.
  • Full PLA+ shell printed on three Ultimaker printers.
  • Repurposed mannequin torso + aluminium spine.
  • Off-the-shelf BLDC motors & 3D printed Wheels.
  • RPLidar A3 + SLAM → ≈ 5 cm indoor accuracy.
  • Samsung Galaxy Tab A 10.1″ used as chest display.
  • Off-shelf LED Mask.

Field history

  • 2021 launch – first demo at Sharjah Research, Technology & Innovation Park.
  • Appeared on stage at a UAE Conferences (2022).
  • Roamed exhibition floors in a school tech fair.
  • Featured in Sharjah News Instgram and TV Channel (see video below).
  • Called on stage graduating students during their graduation ceremony.
